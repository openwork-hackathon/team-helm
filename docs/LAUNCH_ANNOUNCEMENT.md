## Economic Model

### Sustainability

We need ~60 paid users to break even on API costs. At 300+ users, we're profitable and can fund development.

**Current monthly costs:**
- OpenAI API: ~$50-100
- Vercel KV: ~$20
- Vercel Hosting: ~$20
- **Total: ~$120-170/month**

**Revenue streams:**
- 3% mint royalty on every buy
- 3% burn royalty on every sell
- Protocol fees from premium features
- 1% of fees burned (deflationary)

### No Pre-mine, Fair Launch

- **No VC allocation**
- **No team pre-mine**
- **No insider discounts**
- Everyone buys from the same bonding curve

We earn through royalties as the product grows. Skin in the game.
